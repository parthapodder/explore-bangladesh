Travel Tips for Popular Destinations

Helpful guidance to make your journey safer, enjoyable, and well-planned.

Sundarbans

  • Travel with a registered tour guide for safety.
  • Wear light, full-sleeve clothing to avoid insects.
  • Carry drinking water, first-aid, and mosquito repellent.
  • Avoid roaming alone inside dense forest areas.
  • Best season: November–February.

Sajek Valley

  • Carry warm clothes—nights are cold in the hills.
  • Mobile network is weak, download info beforehand.
  • Avoid traveling at night; hill roads are risky.
  • Respect tribal culture and ask before taking photos.
  • Best views early morning with floating clouds.

Sitakunda

  • Wear trekking shoes; trails can get slippery.
  • Start early morning to avoid heat during hikes.
  • Carry extra drinking water.
  • Avoid waterfalls during heavy rain (very dangerous).
  • Keep electronics waterproofed.

Bandarban

  • Hire an experienced driver for hill roads.
  • Carry warm clothes for cold nights.
  • Wear trekking shoes for hill trails.
  • Respect local tribal culture and rules.
  • Best time to visit: October–March.

Cox's Bazar

  • Respect sea safety flags—avoid swimming on red flag days.
  • Do not enter the sea after dark.
  • Protect belongings at the busy beach area.
  • Visit Inani or Himchori for quieter beaches.
  • Apply sunscreen frequently.

Jaflong

  • Wear shoes suitable for walking on stones.
  • Visit early morning for clear-water views.
  • Avoid deep areas—currents can change suddenly.
  • Check weather (upstream rain raises water level).
  • Respect tribal village rules.

Saint Martin's Island

  • Avoid sea travel during storms—boats may get canceled.
  • Return early if not staying overnight.
  • Do not walk on coral (protect the reef).
  • Carry cash; mobile network is limited.
  • Best time: November–February.

Sreemangal

  • Visit tea gardens early morning for best views.
  • Hire a guide for Lawachara National Park trails.
  • Stay quiet to spot wildlife and birds.
  • Try Seven-Layer Tea from a hygienic shop.
  • Rent a bicycle for easy garden exploration.
